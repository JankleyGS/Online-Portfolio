Buddha on Red Berries

For this piece I used Adobe Photoshop. I took a picture of a small Buddha statue I had laying around and placed it on top of some red berries I photographed at Cal State East Bay. Then I used a customized set of brushes with a water-splash stroke. I wanted to give the idea that the Buddah was causing the berries to morph into a liquid state of matter. Basically, it's making juice.

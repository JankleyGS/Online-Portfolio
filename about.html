About Me

I’m a multimedia visual artist based in the Bay Area, and a recent graduate from California State University East Bay. I focus on illustration and branding, and love working on projects that are both thought provoking and aesthetically pleasing. I enjoy creating unique ideas that engage the public eye and leaves it hungry for more.

If you are interested in making a statement and are not afraid to be loud, contact me and we’ll make it happen.
